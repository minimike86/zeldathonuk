What is Zeldathon?
Zeldathon is a group of Zelda fans, artists, musicians, streamers, and all-around good people coming together to have fun and raise money for charity!

We're based in the United Kingdom. There are other Zelda Marathon's run world-wide! Please check out these wonderful people as well:

  • Zeldathon (USA) zeldathon.com
  • Zelda Dungeon (USA) zeldadungeon.net
  • Zelda Speed Runs (USA) zeldaspeedruns.com
  • Rupeethon (USA) rupeethon.org
  • Hyrule Hustlers (USA) twitter.com/hyrulehustlers
  • Zeldathon (France) zeldathon.fr

How can I help support the cause?
Thank you in advance for supporting our mission. You can do any of the following to support what we do:
  • Watch the stream
  • Spread the word on social media
  • Donate during an event
  • Donate art or prizes
  • Sponsor one of our events
Where do my donations go?
Your donations are processed through either Facebook Fundraising, Tiltify, or JustGiving and go straight to the charity. Zeldathon does not touch a single penny, we just record the total donated.
When can I donate?
We usually open the donation pages a month or two before the stream and close donations one month after we stop streaming.
Can I ask more questions?
Join us on Discord to ask more questions and hang out with the team!
